# Core Concepts

## Chess Is the Foundation

Standard chess rules apply unless a card or special rule says otherwise.

## Cards Create Exceptions

Cards do not replace chess. They create specific exceptions, additions, or temporary changes.

## Determinism

Once decks are shuffled and hands are drawn, the game should produce one clear result from the same sequence of decisions.

## Ownership

Cards belong to the player whose deck included them.

## Control

The player who currently uses a card or piece may control it. Control can change during play. Ownership does not change unless a rule says it does.

## Public and Hidden Information

- Public information may be inspected by both players at all times.
- Hidden information is limited to cards in hand, deck order, and face-down Trap cards unless a card says otherwise.

## Zones

Cards may move between the deck, hand, battlefield, and graveyard. Some future rules may create additional zones.
