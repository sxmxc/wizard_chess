# Glossary

- **Active player**: The player taking the current turn.
- **Battlefield**: The board and its occupied squares.
- **Card ownership**: Which player's deck a card came from.
- **Card control**: Which player currently uses a card or piece.
- **Graveyard**: The zone for discarded, destroyed, or resolved cards unless a card says otherwise.
- **Hidden information**: Information one player may not inspect at all times.
- **Legal target**: A target that satisfies the card's requirements.
- **Mana**: The resource used to play cards.
- **Match format**: The agreed ruleset for the game being played.
- **Priority**: The right to act when a timing window is open.
- **School**: A card's magical discipline.
- **Unit**: A card that attaches to a chess piece.
